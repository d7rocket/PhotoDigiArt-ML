- ClipExtractor(BaseExtractor) with lazy ONNX loading matching DepthExtractor pattern exactly

- extract() returns ExtractionResult(extractor_name="semantic", data={"mood_tags": [...], "object_tags": [...]}, arrays={"embedding": np.ndarray})
